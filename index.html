Abstract

We propose Roboseek, a novel framework that integrates reinforcement learning (RL) with the cross-entropy method (CEM) to improve embodied agents in complex multi-step tasks. Our approach enables agents to efficiently explore high-dimensional action spaces while ensuring robust generalization to real-world environments. Extensive experiments in both simulation and real-world robotics validate the effectiveness of our method.
